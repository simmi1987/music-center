Sunday 24th March 2019, 2pm

Die Frankfurter Oompah Band

Carlton Theatre - Carlyle Gardens

Guten Tag!
Embark on a cultural journey with Die Frankfurter Oompah Band, as they perform the distinctive music of Germany and Bavaria. The concert will include traditional Volkfest Music, beer-garden favorites, and a few modern classics.
$25 Adult - $20 Concession - Music Centre Members $15
Free admission for children 12 and under
